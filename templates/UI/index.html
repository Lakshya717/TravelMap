Why

College TravelMap was born from a simple idea: traveling to and from college shouldn't be so complicated.

Every semester, thousands of students travel long distances between their homes and campus. We saw our friends and classmates struggling to coordinate, relying on messy, disconnected group chats to find co-travelers and share rides.

We built this dynamic, social mapping application specifically for college students to fix that. It's a central hub to simplify these journeys. Our platform helps you find groups, share rides between cities, and see where other students from your college are while in transit.

Instead of chaos, you can create, share, and join travel plans directly on a live map, making it easy to form groups, share rides, and meet up. This is travel, coordinated.
